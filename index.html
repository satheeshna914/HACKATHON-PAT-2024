Welcome to Navsanjeevan Social Trust

Navsanjeevan Social Trust is a Right based and behavior-change organization, working on solving the most complex problems in Indian society. It crafts and implements developmental processes, training and technology, and capability building of community members, women, and youth. Partnering with State Governments, Local NGO,s and domain experts, Navsanjeevan Social Trust operates in 51 villages, works in 10 schools, and engages with 4136 community members and 665 school students.
